✦ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Elite Members?
Elite Members is Africa's premier community platform for creators, traders, and entrepreneurs. It combines a social feed, live trading signals, a marketplace, creator monetization tools, and a powerful business network — all in one mobile-first app.
Is it really free to join?
Yes! The Free plan gives you access to the community feed, a member profile, and 3 channel subscriptions at no cost. You can upgrade to Elite or Business whenever you're ready to unlock advanced features.
How do the trading signals work?
Elite and Business members get real-time access to verified trading signals from experienced traders. Each signal includes the asset pair, direction (Buy/Sell), entry price, take profit targets, and stop loss levels. Signals cover Forex, Gold, and major cryptocurrencies.
How can I make money as a creator on Elite Members?
Elite Members members can earn through multiple streams: selling courses, ebooks, and digital products on the marketplace; charging for premium signal channels; receiving tips from followers; offering freelance services; and running paid collaborations with other creators or brands.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, Visa, and Mastercard through our secure Pesapal integration. Payments are encrypted and processed securely. We're expanding to more African mobile money providers soon.
Is Elite Members only for Ugandans?
Not at all! Elite Members is a Pan-African platform with members from Uganda, Kenya, Ghana, Nigeria, Tanzania, Zambia, Egypt, and many more countries — as well as African diaspora members worldwide. We're built for Africa and the world.
Can I cancel my membership anytime?
Absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your monthly or annual subscription at any time from your account settings. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.
Is my personal and financial data safe?
Yes. We take security seriously. All payments are processed through PCI-compliant Pesapal. Your personal data is never sold to third parties. We follow strict data protection policies and use industry-standard encryption across the entire platform.
